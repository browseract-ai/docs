### How Credits Work

**Basic formula:** 1 action = 5 credits

**Standard actions (5 credits each):**

- Navigate to page
- Click element
- Input text
- Extract data
- Wait for element
- Go back
- Scroll

**Special actions:**

- Loop: loop count × 5
- Loop List: item count × 5
- Scroll to element: scroll count × 5

---

### Credit Deduction Priority

**Your credits used in this order:**

1. **Daily Bonus** (used first)
   1. Earned via daily login
   2. Valid today only (resets at 00:00)
1. **Monthly Credits** (used second)
   1. From subscriptions/AppSumo
   2. Valid current month only
2. **Permanent Credits** (used last)
   1. From top-ups/rewards
   2. Never expire

Why? To minimize waste of your most valuable credits.

### Claim Daily Credits

**Free users:**

- Log in daily
- Go to **Balance** card (bottom left)
- Click **"Claim Daily Credits"**

**Paid users:**

- Auto-receive 500 credits daily at 00:00 AM (your timezone)
- No action needed!

⏰ Remember: Daily credits expire at 00:00 AM next day—use them!

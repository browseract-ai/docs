## Welcome to BrowserAct

BrowserAct is an **AI-powered** web automation platform designed to transform your browser into a scalable digital workforce.

It enables you to convert repetitive manual web operations—such as data scraping, market monitoring, or form automation—into reliable, automated processes without the need for complex coding.
